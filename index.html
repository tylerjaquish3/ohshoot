Who We Are

Oh Shoot Photo Booth in Spokane was born in September of 2014 when we came across the idea to do a photo both for our friend's wedding. We loved it so much we decided to offer it to everyone!

We strive to win you over with our service and provide the best experience for your event. We can customize every detail of our booth including the backdrop, photo strip logo and even the music playing in the booth. We coordinate every fine detail before the event so there won't be any surprises on the big day.

Contact us today to get started with the perfect package for your event!
